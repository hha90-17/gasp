The 6th convocation of the Eastern African Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. it is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research, and professional development.
This year’s convocation has selected the topic “The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania” as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year’s 18 of November African Statistics Day. The theme states “Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AFCTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development”.

About

The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC) is an institution based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, dedicated to providing high-quality statistical training and capacity-building programs to professionals in the Eastern Africa region. Here are some key points about the college:
Mission: The mission of EASTC is to enhance the capacity of statisticians, economists, researchers, and other professionals in the region through specialized training programs, workshops, and seminars.
Establishment: EASTC was established in 1965 under the auspices of the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa (UNECA) and the Government of Tanzania. It was established to address the need for skilled statisticians and data professionals to support national development efforts in Eastern Africa.
Focus Areas: The college offers programs and courses in various areas of statistics, data analysis, survey methodology, and related fields. It focuses on building the technical skills and competencies required for effective data collection, analysis, and interpretation.
